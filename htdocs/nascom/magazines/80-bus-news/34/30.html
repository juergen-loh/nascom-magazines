CLIMAX PROBLEMS (No it’s not that type of Agony column) –

It has come to my notice that somebody has problems in trying to use the Climax card in a Gemini Network system. After initial suspicions of the software, it was finally realised that some Network systems had been shipped fitted with a particular model of power-supply which did not provide a-5v supply – a fact that didn’t matter with the supplied boards (GM813 CPU + RAM, IVC, etc). The Climax, however, uses 4116 RAMS (which require a –5V supply), and so when this oversight was rectified.... This struck a chord with me, because I can remember doing something similar several years ago. I hadnt bothered to wire the -l12v supply through the backplane because I didn’t use it, and then some months later came to connect a serial printer to the RS232 interface....
