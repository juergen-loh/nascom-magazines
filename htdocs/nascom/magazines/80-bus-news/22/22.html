Finally, I disagree with David Hunt’s comments about XON/XOFF protocol. At work I am user of small computers (mainly ), which use only this protocol. Both in terms of cabling costs and interface simplicity it is greatly to be preffered to handshaking with DTR etc. Indeed, few DEC interfaces support handshake lines, and XON/XOFF is the only available method for 20mA current loop communication.
