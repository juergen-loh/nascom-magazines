The Microvalue Group was formed in 1980 to offer complete support for Nascom products. The group has extended from this original base and now offers a wide range of other products, including the Gemini Multiboard range. All of the items in the Autumn Gemini Catalogue, included with this issue of 80-Bus News, are available from members

of the Microvalue Group.

Other products stocked include a wide range of dot matrix and daisy wheel printers, monitors, Sinclair products, including the ZX81, Dragon and Sharp ranges of computers. All members can offer repair and back-up facilities for both the Nascom and Gemini

products.
