Syncs from TPG on the AVC should be wired (via an isolating capacitor) to pin @ of the socket that the 1.f. aodule plugs into (marked as PL&).

The Red, Green and Blue outputs should be wired, via isolating capacitors) to PLIB pins 4, 3 and 2 respectively. A supply of 5 volts should be taken from the computer via a 470R resistor and wired to PLIG pin & (pin S on the TK1O). This will supply about 3 valts to the decoder IC and internally switch it from video from the i.f. panel to the data inputs. Pin 1 of PLIG is earth. These connections are shown in the diagram below.

For the few that may need their TX set to watch television programmes, it could be arranged to switch the AVC sync and the L.f. video output at PLA with a CHOS switch operated by the 5 volt supply from the Nasco and, together with this supply also switching the decoder IC between video input and data inputs, an automatic changeover from TV to computer when the computer was Plugged in could easily be achieved.

A very important point – before any work of this type is done, the TV set must be isolated from the matns. This not only protects you but’ also the computer.
