Now you can read the time in the addresses 0C82H-0C83H and time differences can be measured by subtracting two time values. As TP1 gives 300Hz, you can measure time differences of up to 3.5 minutes with an accuracy of 1/300 of a second. You can reset the timer at any time, either from Nas-Sys (using the M command) or from BASIC (using DOKE). The timer cannot be used under Nas-Sys 1 as this is not interruptable.
