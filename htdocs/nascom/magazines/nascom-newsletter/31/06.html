E –
calls the Edit mode. A particular dot may be set or cleared with the 1 and 0 keys respectively, the current position being indicated by a flashing cursor. The cursor will automatically advance to the next location when a dot is set or reset. However, this auto cursor advance facility may be disabled and enabled by pressing the A key in the Edit mode. This allows lines and other shapes to be formed easily without having to Backspace the cursor, the cursor keys may be used. When in Edit mode other commands may not be entered. ESC exits the Edit mode.
