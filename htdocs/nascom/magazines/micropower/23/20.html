Now for the back-up supply. This is best done by reference to diagram 2 where the normal SV supply can be obtained from the test pins near the bus connector and where the mew SV supply is connected to the new RAMS and pull-up resistors ONLY. (It is warth noting that another HMe116 could be fitted in place of the Monitor and Video ROM/RAMSs and, by the connection of the back-up supply and the addition of Write strobes, you would be able to change Monitors and Video/Graphics at will and not loose their contents on power down, therefore mimicking the present ROMs. Write Inhibit switching 15 recommended as there can be no guarantee that spurious writes to the Cmos RAM wil not occur during power up and power down.
