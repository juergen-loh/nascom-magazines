LEVEL A BASIC

A 2K BASIC Interpreter for the unexpanded Nascom 1

Level A BASIC has been written to give users of Nascom 1 systems the ease of programming in BASIC, without the need for extra memory or expansion boards, It is supplied in two EPROMs, which are plugged into the EPROM sockets normally occupied by the Nascom Nasbug or Nas-Sys monitor program,

LEVEL A Features.

Integer BASIC – range −32767 to +32767
Variables – A to %
Array – single dimension
Functions – ABS, RND, RAM
Arithmetic operators – *, <, /, =, -, < >, +, <=, >, >=
Statements – LIST, RUN, NEW
Commands – REM, LET, PRINT, INPUT, IF, GOTO, GOSUB, RETURN, SAVE, STOP

Level A BASIC was written by CCSOFT, It is supplied in two 2708 EPROMs and comes complete with a user manual that includes fitting instructions and example programs.
